Tricks are what every skateboarder dreams of when he starts skateboarding but getting good at the basics will help you a long way on how well you can control your skateboard i will link down a starter on how to skateboard first then link 5 tricks you can try to learn when you are new and then you have creative freedom

Ollie

This is basis of all tricks before you start anything else this trick helps you to get a feel on how to jump from place without having to leave your skateboard if you are trying to cruise on a skateboard this is a must learn as you can jump from road to curb and curb to road this trick pops your board up without you losing momentum and is what evry skater uses when they are starting out

Frontside 180

This is how you turn yourself around on a skateboard with ease if you have mastered the basic ollie i suggest you move onto this next basic trick to help you unlock full control of your skateboard because it allows you too turn around on your skateboard with out turning your skateboard all the way round it looks flashy and is very useful

Backside 180

It is pretty much the same as front 180 instead your doing it the other way depending what foot you use once you know how to do those tricks you have pretty much got full control of your skateboard and can practice any trick you please

Popshove it

This trick allows you to turn the board full 180 or 360 without you turning your body it is really quickand easy and allows you to get into a fakie position immadeiately not needed if you want to cruise but really fun to do and pull off

Kickflip

This trick is the basis trick you need to learn before you start flipping your skateboard other ways i recommend you do this flip trick first as it is the easiest and the cleanest one for beginners to try and first it realy helps you to understand how the skateboard physics work and after you have mastered this trick you will have full control of your skateboard it isnt necessarily needed if you want to cruise but can look flashy and impress people most of all
